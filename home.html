Frequently Asked Questions

Is SnapTakeoff free to use?
Yes, we offer a generous Free Tier for small projects. You can generate limited estimates and test the AI capabilities without a credit card.
How do I save my progress?
We use a local file system. Click the "Save" button in the toolbar to download a project file (.json). You can "Load" this file anytime to restore your exact measurements.
What file formats do you support?
We support standard image formats (JPG, PNG) and PDF blueprints. For best AI results, ensure high-contrast black and white drawings.
Can I use this on a tablet?
SnapTakeoff is optimized for desktop browsers due to the precision required for mouse clicks, but it can be viewed on tablets for reference.
